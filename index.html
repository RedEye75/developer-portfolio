My Family

I am a part of a middle class family in which there are 4 members. The 4 members include my father and my two 2 sibilings. The head of the family is my father who usually enjoys a commanding position. Any decision taken in the family is verified with him first. No one in the family dares to go against what he says as he is the respected guardian of the family. His decisions are always fair and reasonable in the eyes of everyone and he is never influenced by anyone. My father is a retired professor and he helps me and my sister in our studies in the evening.
